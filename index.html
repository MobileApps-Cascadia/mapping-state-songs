About State Tuned

As part of Cascadia Community College’s Integrative Relay Project connected to the Maps Annual Integrated Learning Theme, over 275 students took up parts of the question of state songs, building upon each other’s work, passing different parts of the project from quarter to quarter, through ten classes, and culminating in the State Tuned Mobile App and Mixed Media Installation.

The State Tuned Mobile App provides the ability to listen to state songs, upload photos to Facebook, locate your state on Google Maps, choose your favorite state songs, and like the application on Facebook.

We encourage you to also visit the State Tuned website.
